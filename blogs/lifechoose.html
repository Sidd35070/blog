The life I choose
TAGS: Choices | Thinking | Motivation

"The closer you are to death, the more alive you feel."

Challenges, fights, fierce-love, knock-downs, the extremes… I want to take life by its throat. If it’s a limited time I have to go through, I won’t just let it pass easily playing a victim. When life stands in front of me, I’ll look into it’s eyes and see myself through. I won’t give in even if the battle is certain to be lost. I’ll fight till the end because that’s the best I can do.

I will dive deep into the moments and extract out the glistening pearls that life has hidden inside of the rocky horizons of its heart.

I’ll fight the worst battles, challenge the best kingdoms, live in the best castles and ride the fastest chariots. I will live in the extremes. I will fight in extreme consciousness and then be lost in the aura it creates.

I will live to live, and will let my heart race faster than it can. It’s not just the rush that I want to live in, but the trance of the stillness inside of that rush that I love.

I choose a war over a bed of roses. I choose to fight and I choose to rule. And I choose to be a
WARRIOR KING.
